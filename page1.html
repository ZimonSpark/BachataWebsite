Who is Simon?

Hi, I'm Simon! Happy to have you reading my wep page.

Bachata has been a love at first dance for me. I remeber vividly when I saw for the first time people dancing in Ooppera outdoor socials . They were so happy, smiling and moving together. I stopped and seated to watch, I wanted to breath in that joy. One dancer came to me and asked "wanna dance?" A week later I was taking classes and bachata has been part of my life ever since.

If you come to my classes, I hope to make you fall in love with bachata in the same way I did.

Simon
