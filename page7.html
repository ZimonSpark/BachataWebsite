Thursday Class

Program from September to December 2025:
• 19:30 - 21:00 Tecnique and Musicality Beginners Lv.2
• Other classes may be coming soon

During the Beginners Lv.2 Tecnique Class you will enlarge your repertoire of social dance moves.
We will insist on the correct use of frame and start observing how to improve the movements you already know.
During the classes we will provide you input for good habits to develop as dancer to speed up your learning curve.

During the Musicality Class we will focus on your ability to recognise the components of bachata sensual songs and hit them correctly.
We'll help you develop your own dance style.

Prerequirements:
at least 1 month of bachata sensual classes or similar experience.

The classes are held in Helsinki Dance Central Studio.

We communicate all info about the courses in this WhatsApp group: https://chat.whatsapp.com/JY2pxtUbDu82sJC2WhbHIv
