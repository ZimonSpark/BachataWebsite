Simon Sensual Bachata

If you are looking for a hobby or activity in Helsinki that will allow you to connect with others, have fun, avoid alcohol, move your body, and develop as a person. Bachata Sensual is perfect for you. I will be happy to introduce you to this fantastic world.

The three pillars of Bachata

Social activity

Bachata is not meant to remain in the classes. You learn to prepare yourself for the real world, bachata parties are everywhere around the globe. With bachata sensual you'll be able to explore.

Conversation

Bachata is a conversation through body movements. To avoid boring monologues, leader and follower listen to each other. Only this way the conversation can be pleasant, and sometime, even mindblowing.

Fun Fun Fun

Bachata is supposed to be fun all the way from the beginning. You don't win a medal to accomplish a move or coreo. Focus on connecting with whom you are dancing rather than looking good.
