Social activity

Bachata is not meant to remain in the classes. You learn to prepare yourself for the real world, bachata parties are everywhere around the globe. With bachata sensual you'll be able to explore.

Fun Fun Fun

Bachata is supposed to be fun all the way from the beginning. You don't win a medal to accomplish a move or coreo. Focus on connecting with whom you are dancing rather than looking good.
